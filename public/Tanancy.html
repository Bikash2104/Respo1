Frequently Asked Questions

What does the cleaning service include?
Our cleaners will follow a list of priorities arranged that will be arranged with you when you make a booking. In case your business has any special cleaning needs, make sure you mention it before the service is booked.
Do I need to provide the cleaning products?
If you need specific cleaning products or materials, please let us know in your booking so that your cleaner can bring them with them for a small fee. Please note that you will need to supply your own cleaning equipment (including a vacuum, mop, and bucket), as cleaners typically travel by public transportation.
Is there a contract?
There is no contract – if you‘re not happy with the cleaning service and want to cancel, just let us know with 24 hours notice. You can also reschedule appointments by calling us on 07943 272 519.
